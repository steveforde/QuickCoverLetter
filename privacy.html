QuickCoverLetter App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

Data We Collect

We collect the following personal information required solely for the functionality of the app and payment processing:

  • Email Address: Collected for the purposes of verifying your one-time purchase and sending transaction receipts.
  • Name, Job Title, Company Name: Collected locally on your device only to generate the cover letter text. This data is **never** sent to our servers, is **not** stored by us, and is cleared when you click 'Clear & Reset'.

Security and Payment

All payment transactions are handled securely by **Stripe**. We do not store or process any sensitive financial information (such as credit card numbers). Data transmitted between the app and our servers (for purchase processing) is encrypted via standard HTTPS/TLS protocols.
